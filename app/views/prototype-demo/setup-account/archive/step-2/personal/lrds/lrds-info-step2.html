Local resident discount scheme (LRDS)

Initial deposit to your account

You need to make an initial minimum deposit of £10 to open your account.

Select the LRDS account you wish to apply for

  • This account allows you to make up to 50 crossings in any one year.
  • You will need to make an initial minimum deposit of £10 to open your account.
  • This amount will be used to pay for the crossings until the LRDS account has been created.
  • You will be responsible to add funds to your account.
  • Once the documents required to create an LRDS account are verified, you have to pay £10 for the LRDS account.
  • After you have made 50 crossings in the year,
    • You can either add £10 to the existing account which will upgrade you to LRDS 20 for the remainder of the year.
    • Or you will have to set up a separate Pre-Pay or Pay as you go account to pay for additional crossings.
  • This account allows you to make unlimited crossings in any one year
  • You will need to make an initial deposit of £10 to open your account.
  • This amount will be used to pay for the crossings until the LRDS account has been created.
  • You will be responsible to add funds to your account.
  • Once the documents required to create an LRDS account are verified, you have to pay £20 for the LRDS account
