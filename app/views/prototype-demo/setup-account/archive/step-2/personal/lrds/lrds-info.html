Local resident discount scheme (LRDS)

This account acts as a personal manual top-up account until the documents are verified and approved. Once approved, the account will get converted into an LRDS account.

  • You can only get discount for one vehicle in an account.
  • You must provide evidence by uploading the documents or photographs. Alternatively you can send them by post.

You will need to provide

  • Your vehicle details
  • A current Council Tax bill that matches the address in your application
  • Proof that you live in Dartford or Thurrock council, e.g. a gas, electricity or water bill (less than 3 months old), bank or credit card statement, (less than 3 months old), income support book, rent document or driving licence
  • Proof that your vehicle is registered in Dartford or Thurrock council, e.g. a copy of your V5C vehicle registration or a vehicle lease in your name
  • A debit or credit card
